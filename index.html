How to Play

Game Rules:
Welcome to T-POP GUESSER! The rules are simple: Guess the correct idol based on information gained.
You have 10 guesses to guess it correctly.
After searching for an idol, click the "Guess" button to submit your guess.
If you guess correctly, you win!
If you run out of guesses, you lose.
A green colored section means that the information is correct.
A yellow colored section means that the information is within the range of 2 or have partial correct.
Enjoy the game and have fun! Tap on screen to continue.

Developer's Note: An idol's Role may be inaccurate.
